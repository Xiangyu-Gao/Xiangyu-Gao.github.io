Xiangyu Gao is a research assistant and Ph.D. candidate in the department of electrical & computer engineering at the University of Washington, under the supervision of Prof. Sumit Roy at FUNLAB. His research advances next-generation radar perception and multi-sensor fusion methods through statistical signal processing and deep learning.

Xiangyu received a B.S. degree in communication engineering from Xidian University, Xi’an, China, in 2018, and an M.S. degree in electrical and computer engineering from the University of Washington, Seattle, WA, USA, in 2020. He is now also pursuing a M.S. degree in Applied Mathematics at the University of Washington.
